Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Hexbound ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile game developed by Artem Osetrov. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Hexbound app ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password hash when you register an account.
  • Game Data: Character progress, inventory, combat history, achievements, and in-game currency balances.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, and general gameplay patterns to improve game balance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the game experience.
  • Authenticate your account and secure your game data.
  • Process in-app purchases and manage your virtual currency.
  • Provide matchmaking and leaderboard features.
  • Send important account-related notifications (e.g., security alerts).
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to improve game balance.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure) with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures including rate limiting, server-side validation, and secure authentication protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting.
  • Apple: In-app purchase processing via the App Store.
  • Vercel: Backend API hosting.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Hexbound is rated for ages 12+ due to simulated gambling content (shell game, fortune wheel). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your in-game profile.
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Update your account information through the app settings.

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All game progress, characters, items, and currency will be permanently removed.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data and game data are permanently deleted from our servers immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notification. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:
